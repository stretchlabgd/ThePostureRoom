Meet Dr Steven Kuan

Dr Steven began his healthcare journey as a sports trainer, working closely with athletes to prevent and manage injuries. His desire to help more people move better and live pain-free led him to complete his Master of Chiropractic at Macquarie University.

Dr Steven has a strong interest in treating musculoskeletal issues such as sports injuries, neck pain, shoulder pain, knee pain, headaches and poor posture.

Dr Steven focuses on treating the root cause of pain and dysfunction, rather than just relieving the symptoms.

His treatment approach combines soft tissue therapy, chiropractic adjustments, and personalized rehab exercises to support long-term recovery and improve everyday movement.

Dr Steven is fluent in English and Mandarin.
